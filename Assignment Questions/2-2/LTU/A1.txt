PART A

Take a text in your mother tongue which is at least 100 years old. Rewrite it in modern version of the same language. Compare, analyse and comment about language changes you observe. You need to give the gloss in English for the discussion of samples, wherever possible. The text should have minimum of 25 sentences and maximum of 30 sentences.

PART B

Select five of your writings in social media post, blog or messages and rewrite in the old version of your languages. Adopt the same style of the text chosen in part A. Give screen shot of your current text. It should have natural flow. You may redact any names for maintain privacy. Describe the language changes that you observe.

 Note: No two students should have the same text.
